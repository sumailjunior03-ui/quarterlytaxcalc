About QuarterlyTaxCalc.com

Purpose of This Calculator

QuarterlyTaxCalc.com provides a browser-based estimate of quarterly U.S. federal tax payments for self-employed income and other untaxed earnings that may require estimated payments.

Who This Tool Is For

This tool is useful for freelancers, contractors, sole proprietors, and anyone trying to estimate quarter-by-quarter federal tax obligations before making estimated payments.

How The Calculator Works

The calculator estimates self-employment tax, applies the half-SE-tax deduction, subtracts the standard deduction for the selected filing status, estimates federal income tax using progressive 2025 brackets, and divides the annual estimate into four equal quarterly payments.

Data Sources and Scope

This calculator uses a deterministic federal-only methodology. It does not include state taxes, special credits, itemized deductions, safe harbor rules, or underpayment penalty calculations.
