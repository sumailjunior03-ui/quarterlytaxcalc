Zero-Data Model

QuarterlyTaxCalc.com is a static, client-side tool. All tax calculations run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No income figures, personal information, or calculation results are sent to any server, stored in any database, or shared with any third party by this site.

We do not operate user accounts, logins, or any form of session storage. When you close or refresh the page, all input data is discarded.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Social Security numbers or taxpayer identification numbers
